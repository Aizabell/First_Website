Frequently asked questions (FAQ)



  Q.   Which countries are available for Delivery Services ?

  A.   We operate our delivery services only in the United States. Unfortunately, we are unable to do shipping service to other countries.


  Q.   How long does it take to arrive ?

  A.   Our delivery service may takes 10-15 work days.


  Q.   Is it possible to get a refund ?

  A.   We only accepts refund when you cancelled the order within 3 days from the day you ordered or the item is defective according to our policy.


  Q.   How much spending needed to get delivery fee ?

  A.   If the total purchase in one order is higher than 1000$ , we offer free delivery charges.


  Q.   How long does it take to restock ?

  A.   It usually takes one week to restock in normal occasion but if it is highly demanded , it will take 2 weeks to restock.
